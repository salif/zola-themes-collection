Cablab Research

The Computational Applications & AI for Biomaterials Laboratory.
This is a simple overview of our lab goals.
More specific goals are in Projects

Double lifespan of humans

Our lab is looking to reduce aging problems and help humans to live into the future by approaching the problem in new ways. We investigate multiple molecular pathways and develop novel computational methods to understand and modify the aging process.

Decrease travel time to Mars

We’re developing new propulsion technologies and optimizing trajectories through advanced computational modeling. Our approach combines novel materials science with AI-driven optimization techniques.
